"Hey Siri..."

...where can I find a good Asian restaurant in Stockholm?

Miyakodori

Upplandsgatan 7

Miyakodori is a vibrant yet relaxed Japanese izakaya. You can pick your favourites from the menu, choose a special from their black board, or leave the choice to your server and go for the omakase. They have a separate bar as well, where you can enjoy drinks as well as eat.

Standout's:
pork belly skewers
chicken liver mousse with milk bread

Lu

Skånegatan 88

Lu serves Cantonese street style food from Hong Kong, in a chaotic yet cosy, canteen like setting. If you are fortunate to live (somewhat) close by, they also offer take away, which means you can enjoy their food even if it's packed.

Standout's:
pork dumplings
beef noodles
